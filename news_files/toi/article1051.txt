Delhi Capitals IPL Team: Complete squad of Delhi Capitals team & players list in IPL 2019
NEW DELHI: Delhi Capitals , previously known as Delhi Daredevils, used their purse of Rs 25.5 crore wisely at the IPL auction to buy the likes of Colin Ingram, Axar Patel and Ishant Sharma, and went home with Rs 7.7 crore in their pocket at the end of the showpiece event. The Delhi outfit bagged veteran Ishant for Rs 1.1 crore before splurging Rs 6.4 crore for South African hard-hitter Colin Ingram.After being released by Kings XI Punjab, Axar Patel got a handsome deal of Rs 5 crore while all-rounder Hanuma Vihari was bought for Rs 2 crore.Off-spinner Jayant Yadav, who was earlier retained by the franchise, will now play for Mumbai Indians in the 2019 IPL after being traded by Delhi Capitals two days after the auction held in Jaipur.
